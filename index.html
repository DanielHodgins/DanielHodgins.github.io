About Me

I am an incoming PHD student in mathematics at Auburn University. I obtained my Master of Science in Mathematics at the University of South Alabama, where I earned my undergraduate degree in Mechanical Engineering and Mathematics with a concentration in Aerospace Engineering in 2023. My research interests currently lie in graph theory and combinatorial game theory. Outside of mathematics, I enjoy camping, tabletop games, and swimming. I also enjoy assisting in math outreach. I currently serve on the steering committee of the JRMF Community Math Circle.
