List of Slang words

This list may have untraditional spellings or usage.

  • gootchie: means good
  • rizz: charisma
  • sus: adj to describe the perception that someone or something did some killing
  • goat: screaming beast
  • g.o.a.t.: greatest of all time
  • saucy: careless and impulsive response
  • salty: acting as if your mouth is full of salt

Southern words

This is a list of words in the southern dialect.

  • chester drawer: a wooden piece of furnture that has many drawers
  • yall: second person plural pronoun
  • poboy: long sandwhich, traditionally stuff with shrimp or chicken
  • devil beating his wife: raining and shining
  • crawdad/crawfish: aquatic critter akin to a lobster but smaller
  • Ain't: are not
  • catty corner: askew
  • spicket: spiget or a plain single valve faucet
  • taters: potatoes, especially when cooked in such a way in that the potatoes are dry
  • that-a-way: phrase when indicating a direction where otheres might say that way
  • buggy: a container on wheels used often for shopping
