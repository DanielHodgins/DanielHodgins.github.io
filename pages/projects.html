DBF Plane 2022-2023

The objective was to create an "electronic warfare" UAV (unmanned aerial vehicle). More specifically for the 2022-2023 academic year our objective was to design, build, and test an aircraft to execute electronic warfare (EW) missions. Flight missions will include staging of the aircraft, surveillance, and jamming. Mockingbird-An Aquatic UAV drone

The Mockingbird is an UAV (unmanned aerial vehicle) plane that could land and take off from water. The Mockingbird also has a very low "wing cube loading". A fun fact is that the fuselage of the Mockingbird is a salad bowl.
